Operational Definitions & Methodologies

  • Average Transit Time
    The average duration measured from the moment the dispatch vehicle clears the Ahafo Boom Gate (Step 4) to the recorded time of arrival at the Tarkwa BCP Laboratory (Step 5). The target transit threshold is strictly ≤ 6.5 hours.
  • Total Turnaround Time
    The end-to-end operational timeframe beginning when samples first arrive at the Ahafo on-site Process Plant lab (Step 1) and concluding upon final receipt logging at the Tarkwa laboratory.
  • Delayed Run (> 6.5h)
    Any individual dispatch run where the Transit Time strictly exceeds the 6.5-hour target. These are visually flagged as red dots on the trend charts and negatively impact an individual driver's compliance score.
  • Overall Compliance Rate
    The percentage of total dispatch runs that successfully completed the route within the 6.5-hour transit target without registering any "Non-Compliant" or "Missing Arrival" critical exceptions.
  • Incident Log Severity
    🔴 Critical: Non-compliant runs, severe logistical breakdowns, or missing arrival confirmations.
    🟠 Warning: Delayed runs (over 6.5h), severe traffic, weather delays, or checkpoint holds.
    🔵 Info: General operational notes or batch inventory inclusions that do not directly fail compliance.
